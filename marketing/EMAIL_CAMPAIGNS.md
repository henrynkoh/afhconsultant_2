### Design Elements
- **Colors**: Primary blue (#0ea5e9), white, gray
- **Fonts**: Arial, sans-serif (web-safe)
- **Images**: Optimized, alt text included
- **Buttons**: Clear CTAs with sufficient padding
- **Mobile**: Responsive design, single column

## Compliance & Best Practices

### CAN-SPAM Compliance
- Clear sender identification
- Accurate subject lines
- Physical address included
- Unsubscribe link prominent
- Honor unsubscribe requests within 10 days

### GDPR Compliance (if applicable)
- Clear consent language
- Easy opt-out process
- Data protection notice
- Right to access/deletion

### Best Practices
- Send from recognizable address
- Use consistent branding
- Test before sending
- Monitor deliverability
- Clean email list regularly
- Segment audiences
- Personalize when possible
