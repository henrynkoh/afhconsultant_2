## Newsletter Best Practices

### Subject Lines
- Keep under 50 characters
- Use numbers and emojis sparingly
- Create urgency or curiosity
- Personalize when possible

### Content
- Keep paragraphs short (2-3 sentences)
- Use bullet points and lists
- Include clear CTAs
- Mobile-friendly design

### Timing
- Tuesday-Thursday: Best open rates
- 10 AM - 2 PM: Optimal send times
- Avoid Monday mornings and Friday afternoons

### Frequency
- Weekly: For study tips and updates
- Bi-weekly: For general content
- Monthly: For major announcements

### Segmentation
- New subscribers: Welcome series
- Active users: Study tips
- Inactive users: Re-engagement campaigns
- Certified consultants: Advanced content
