## Best Practices

1. **Be Conversational**: Threads is text-focused, so write naturally
2. **Respond Quickly**: Engage with comments within hours
3. **Ask Questions**: Drive discussion and engagement
4. **Share Value**: Educational content performs best
5. **Be Authentic**: Show personality and expertise
6. **Build Community**: Support others, not just promote yourself

## Crisis Management

### If Negative Comments:
- Respond professionally
- Address concerns directly
- Offer to continue conversation privately
- Don't delete unless spam/hateful

### If Misinformation:
- Correct politely with facts
- Provide sources (WAC citations)
- Don't be confrontational
- Build trust through accuracy
